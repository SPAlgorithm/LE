LESecure AI – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the LESecure AI application, including its WhatsApp Business API integration, Telegram bot, and related services (collectively referred to as the “App”), operated by LESecure AI (“we”, “our”, or “us”).

About the App

App Name: LESecure AI
Service Description: Quantum-safe encryption and secure message processing on user request
Supported Platforms: WhatsApp, Telegram
Official Website: https://lesecure.ai

Scope of Service

LESecure AI provides security and encryption-related functionality exclusively in response to user-initiated messages on supported messaging platforms. The App does not initiate conversations, send unsolicited messages, or distribute marketing, promotional, or bulk communications.

Information We Process

When a user interacts with the App, we may process:

  • Phone number or platform identifier (required for message delivery and authentication)
  • Message content voluntarily submitted by the user for encryption or decryption
  • Minimal technical metadata necessary to operate and secure the service

How We Use Information

Information is processed solely to fulfill the user’s explicit request, such as encryption, decryption, PIN/password/OTP validation, time-based access controls, and secure response delivery. LESecure AI does not analyze, profile, sell, or reuse message content for advertising, marketing, or any secondary purpose.

Data Sharing

LESecure AI does not sell or rent personal data. Data is shared only with the relevant messaging platform (such as WhatsApp or Telegram) strictly as required to deliver the requested service, in accordance with their respective policies.

Data Retention

Message content is processed transiently and is not stored beyond the duration required to complete the requested operation, unless explicitly stated for a specific feature or required by law.

User Rights

Users may request information about data handling practices or request deletion of applicable data by contacting us using the details provided below.

Policy Compliance

LESecure AI operates in compliance with the WhatsApp Business Messaging Policy, Telegram platform requirements, and applicable data protection laws.
